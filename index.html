Common questions

How do I give a star?
Open a child's jar and tap "Add a Star," then pick a reason.

How do I change the goal or reward?
Tap the gear icon and enter your parent passcode (you set it the first time).

What happens when the jar is full?
Tap "Give reward now" in the parent controls to empty the jar and start over.

Where is my data?
Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
