Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Star Jar is published by Palm Point Ventures. This app is built to need as little of your information as possible — in fact, none of it leaves your device.

Information we collect

We collect nothing. Star Jar has no accounts, no sign-up, and no analytics. The children's names, avatars, stars, goals, rewards, and settings you enter are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Data sharing

Because we never collect your data, we have nothing to share, sell, or disclose.

Third-party services

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no tracking, and makes no network requests for your data.

Children's privacy

Star Jar is designed for parents and families. Since no personal information is collected or transmitted, the app does not knowingly gather data from anyone, including children under 13.

Deleting your data

All data lives on your device. Deleting the app removes everything permanently.
